Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wingbound ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile game application ("the App").

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure compatibility.
  • Gameplay Data: Scores, progress, achievements, and in-game activity to power leaderboards and cloud saves.
  • Analytics Data: App usage patterns and crash reports to improve performance and fix issues.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience.
  • To operate leaderboards and competitive features.
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues.
  • To send optional game updates and notifications (if you opt in).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with analytics partners. We do not share identifiable data with third parties except as required by law.

Wingbound is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

6. Data Retention

We retain gameplay and analytics data for up to 12 months, after which it is deleted or anonymized.
